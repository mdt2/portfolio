Case study: Meal prep UX design

My Meals was created to satisfy an assignment for a user experience course. The objective was to wireframe and mock up five pages of a hypothetical product user experience flow.

The user begins on the landing page that outlines the benefits of the product. From there, they can explore pricing options on the subscription page. Below the subscription options is a comparison chart to help users differentiate between plans. After the user chooses a plan, they are directed to the account creation page and then to their settings. Within the settings page, they can choose diet restrictions and food preferences that will customize and personalize their meal generation on the following page.
